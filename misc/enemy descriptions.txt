Common Angel
Simple-minded soldier that uses a dangerous mace for mobility. Seemingly dispatched in groups as blockades.

Oversized Angel
Nonviolent while resting. It retaliates with a lunge followed by a large explosion.

Sneezy Angel
Patrol unit. Seemingly allergic to the atmosphere. Its powerful propeller can rupture ear drums.

Unorthodox Angel
Highly mobile and dangerous. Its weight and agility allow it to unleash powerful shockwaves.

Wonderful Angel
Skilled archer of poisonous arrows. Its high agility may be a result of exposure to poison.
